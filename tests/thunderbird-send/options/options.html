Thunderbird Send

Options

This is your global options/preferences for Thunderbird Send. For the account management pages, go to the Thunderbird Menu (≡) > ⚙️ Settings > ✏️ Composition > Attachments.
  • Use compose action popups in the compose window instead of standalone popup windows. Requires Thunderbird 115 or greater to automatically open the compose action popups.
  • Adds a “Learn more about” link for the Send service instance to footer of messages.
